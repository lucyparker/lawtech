Edinburgh Facts

Edinburgh was founded a long time ago. Its castle is built on a volcano. Lots of buildings in Edinburgh are very haunted, you can take a ghost tour. But remember, beware of malevolant spirits! A very important thing to remember is that the bus costs £1.70 and they don't give change, so make sure you've got the right money.
